## How to Contribute

1. **Fork the repository** and create your branch from `main`.
2. **Write clear, concise commit messages.**
3. **Test your code** before submitting a pull request.
4. **Open a pull request** with a clear description of your changes.
5. **Address review comments** as needed.

## Code of Conduct

Be respectful and inclusive. Discriminatory or abusive behavior will not be tolerated.

## Reporting Issues

If you find a bug or have a suggestion, please open an issue and provide details.
